Página Informativa – HGC

¿Quiénes Somos?HGC es una oficina de servicios administrativos que trabaja con transparencia, responsabilidad y apego a la ley. No somos abogados ni brindamos asesoría legal, pero apoyamos a nuestros clientes en la gestión documental y en trámites administrativos de manera clara y segura.

Aviso Legal “I AM NOT AN ATTORNEY LICENSED TO PRACTICE LAW. I MAY NOT GIVE LEGAL ADVICE OR ACCEPT FEES FOR LEGAL ADVICE. I AM NOT ACCREDITED TO REPRESENT YOU IN IMMIGRATION MATTERS.”

Políticas y Avisos

📌 Office Policies – HGC 1. Transparency and Legality - HGC always acts in accordance with the laws of the State of Florida, including Chapter 117 Florida Statutes – Notaries Public. - Legal advice or immigration representation is not provided, as established by law. - All services are carried out transparently, with written records and clear receipts of the processes performed. 2. Delivery and Custody of Documents - All documents received will be delivered only to their rightful owner. - The office does not retain immigration or personal documents once they have been processed. - Upon request, clients may receive copies of the delivered documents for their records. - An administrative control file may be maintained only for administrative purposes and does not constitute undue retention. 3. Client Communication - All clients must maintain active communication via email during ongoing processes. - Physical documents will only be sent through certified courier services (UPS or equivalent). - The client is responsible for covering shipping and handling costs. - The office is not responsible for delays caused by third parties (postal or courier services). 4. Customer Service and Non-Discrimination - This office maintains respectful, dignified, and professional treatment of all clients. - Discrimination is not permitted based on race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, nationality, age, disability, or immigration status. 5. Fees and Charges - Notary fees will be charged in accordance with Florida State law. - Any additional services will be disclosed to the client before being provided. - A receipt will be issued for all services performed. 6. Security and Confidentiality - All information and documents received are handled under strict confidentiality. - This office is equipped with security cameras for the protection of clients and staff. - Threats, intimidation, or acts of violence will not be tolerated. 7. Protection Against Threats - The office is protected under: - Florida Statutes § 836.05 – Extortion/Threats 📌 Legal Notice for Office Display 📌 Important I AM NOT AN ATTORNEY LICENSED TO PRACTICE LAW. I MAY NOT GIVE LEGAL ADVICE OR ACCEPT FEES FOR LEGAL ADVICE. I AM NOT ACCREDITED TO REPRESENT YOU IN IMMIGRATION MATTERS. 📌 Legal Protection This office is protected under the following laws: - Florida Statutes Chapter 117 – Notaries Public (Regulation of notaries). - Florida Statutes § 836.05 – Extortion/Threats (Prohibits threats and extortion). - Florida Statutes § 784.048 – Stalking / Harassment (Covers harassment, stalking, and repeated threats, including electronic communications). - First Amendment & Due Process – U.S. Constitution (Protection against unfounded accusations and the right to due process). Any written threat, email, or message may be retained and used as legal evidence. 📌 Document Policy - All documents received will be delivered only to their rightful owner. - This office does not retain immigration or personal documents once they have been notified to the client. - Clients may request a copy of delivered documents at any time. 📌 Fees Notary services are charged in accordance with Florida State law.
